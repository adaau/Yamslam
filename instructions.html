Yamslam Instructions

Quick Rules

Let the five dice fly to achieve the best scoring combination and win the corresponding chip.

There are only 4 chips of each kind. Try to get the best chips before they run out.

Be sure to check out the various bonuses available to get extra points!

Playing the Game

Roll the five dice up to three times to achieve one of eight possible combinations.

After the first and second roll, click on the dice you wish to keep and roll the remaining dice again.

Stop rolling the dice at any time if you achieve a satisfactory combination.

Collecting Chips

There are only four chips of each kind. Grab the best ones before they run out!

If any of your three rolls matches one of the combinations, click on a corresponding chip and end your turn. If a roll satisfies more than one combination, pick the chip of your choice.

If you roll a Yamslam (five dice showing the same face), take one chip of your choice and play again.

If your dice do not match an available chip, do not collect.

Winning the Game

The game ends when the last chip is taken.

At the end of the game, the scoreboard will tally up the points of your chips and add any achieved bonuses to your chip total. The player with the highest total wins.
